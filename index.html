Blackjack Rules

  • Goal: Beat the dealer without going over 21
  • Card values: Number cards = face value, Face cards = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 1.5x
  • Dealer must hit until 17 or higher
  • Tie results in a push (bet returned)
